Quadra Quest II Rules Book

Objective:
The first player to reach the 100th cell on the board wins the game.
Setup:
  1. Players: The game can be played by 2 to 6 players.
  2. Board: The board is a grid with 100 cells, labeled from 1 to 100.
  3. Tokens: Each player has a unique token which starts at cell 1.
  4. Zones:
    • Danger Zones: 20 cells where players may face setbacks.
    • Safe Zones: 10 cells where players gain temporary protection from danger zones.
Starting the Game:
  1. Number of Players: Choose the number of players (between 2 and 6).
  2. Player Names: Enter names for each player. Default names (p1, p2, etc.) can be used if no names are provided.
  3. Game Start: Press the "Start Game" button to initialize the board.
Taking Turns:
  1. Roll Dice: On your turn, press the "Roll Dice" button to roll a 6-sided die.
  2. Move Token: Move your token forward by the number of spaces shown on the die.
    • If the token lands on or passes the last cell (100), the move is invalid unless the exact number to reach 100 is rolled.
  3. Zone Effects: After moving, apply the effects of any zone you land on.
    • Danger Zones (Red Cells):
      • If you land on a danger zone and do not have protection, you must move back by a randomly determined number of steps (1 to 6).
      • If you have protection, the danger zone is negated, and you lose one protection.
    • Safe Zones (Blue Cells):
      • The first time you land on a safe zone, you gain one-time protection from danger zones.
      • If you land on another safe zone consecutively without hitting a danger zone in between, all safe zone benefits are canceled.
Special Rules:
  • Protection: Protection can only negate the effect of a danger zone once.
  • Consecutive Safe Zones: Hitting consecutive safe zones without hitting a danger zone will cancel all current and future safe zone benefits for that player.
  • Carry-Over Steps: If a playerâ€™s movement is reduced to a negative position due to danger zone setbacks, the extra steps are carried over and deducted from the next dice roll.
Winning the Game:
  • The first player to land exactly on the 100th cell wins the game.
  • If the rolled number is too high to land exactly on 100, the player stays in place.
Game End:
  • The game ends immediately when a player reaches the 100th cell.
  • The "Roll Dice" button will be disabled, and a "Restart Game" button will appear to start a new game.
Restarting the Game:
  • Press the "Restart Game" button to reset the game and start over.
